BATTLESHIP



User ships

Computer ships









Place your battleships in the "User ships" area by clicking the mouse. You should install following configuration: 1 x four-deck ship; 2 x three-deck ship; 3 x two-deck ship; 4 x one-deck ship. Leave at least one cell between the different ships. When you are ready press the button "Let's start the game!" and fire first. Turn on the sound. Good luck!
